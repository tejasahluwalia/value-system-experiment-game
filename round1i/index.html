Round 1 Instructions

You will be shown 6 cards on the screen placed in a circular form.

In a clockwise manner you have to discard the cards in the following fashion:

  • Drag all face cards to the top of the screen.
  • Drag all number cards to the bottom of the screen.

For correct discarding you will receive +10 points and for wrong discarding you will receive -10 points.

You have to complete the task in the given time or else you will lose 50 points.

Your goal is to earn maximum points.
